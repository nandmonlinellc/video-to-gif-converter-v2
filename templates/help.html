Help / FAQ

How do I convert a video to GIF?

Upload your video, set your options, and click Convert to GIF. Your GIF will be ready in a few moments.

What video formats are supported?

MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WEBM are supported.

Why is my GIF not showing text?

Check your text overlay options and font selection. If the problem persists, contact support.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum upload size is 50MB per video.
